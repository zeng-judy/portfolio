How the CTA 201 Bus Ridership Story Was Produced

March 3, 2025

To produce this story, I used data from the Chicago Data Portal on CTA Ridership Levels. As of March 3rd, 2025, the most recent ridership levels data is recorded for December 2024.

I exported the 201 bus route data inclusive from March 1, 2023 to December 1, 2024 and imported it into this spreadsheet. Then I verified that Sunday ridership is indeed zero because the 201 bus doesn't operate on Sundays. The month total was also indeed the sum of the two weekday and Saturday ridership.

Since bus ridership varies by month, a double line chart comparing 2023 and 2024 data would highlight the effect of the program on the difference in ridership. I took the data from March through December of each year to leave a sizeable gap in the months before and after the Northwestern 201 ventra program ended on June 30, 2024.
